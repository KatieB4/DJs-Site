How it works

The DJ's Process

01

Initial Phone Meeting

Conducted by our in house team of remodeling experts. On the call, we will discuss project scope, budget and time frame.

02

Free In-Home Consultation

A DJ's professional will meet you in your home to better understand your project vision, discuss budget, and take preliminary measurements.

03

Proposal

Following initial consultations, we provide a proposal for the cost of materials and labor needed to make your project a reality.

04

Preparation

Upon your approval, DJ's begins preparing necessary plans, digital designs, required permits, and your chosen material orders through our pre-qualified suppliers.

05

Scheduling

We schedule an approximate start date after all materials have been ordered and product delivery dates are established.

06

Construction

Our goal is your to ensure your project runs smoothly and efficiently. We share project details with you every step of the way via our client portal (Buildertrend- video link popup on desktop), so you can relax knowing your project is in good hands!
